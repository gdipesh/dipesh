About

A developer who grew up coding between school and the fields of Jhapa.

I was born and raised in Garamani, Jhapa, in eastern Nepal, and studied at Shree Durga Secondary School. Outside the classroom, I taught myself web design and development one project at a time — starting with simple static pages and gradually picking up HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and now React.js.

I care about websites that load fast, read clearly, and work well on the small, often slower phones that most people in Nepal actually browse on. That focus on simplicity and speed shapes everything I build.
